About

I work at the edge
where data meets decisions.

I am a data systems specialist, econometrician, and statistical educator. My work sits at the intersection of rigorous methodology and practical results.

A
My philosophy
"Most data problems are not really data problems — they are clarity problems."

When a pipeline is broken, when a model keeps getting rejected, when someone cannot explain their own results — the issue is rarely the data itself. It is the gap between what the data is saying and what the person working with it understands.

That gap is exactly where I work. I bring the technical depth to build robust systems and models, and the patience to make sure the people using them actually understand what they have in front of them.

Areas of expertise
Data Systems & Infrastructure
Designing and building data pipelines, databases, and automated reporting systems that are clean, reliable, and built to scale.
Applied Econometrics
Panel data, time-series, causal inference, and regression diagnostics. Rigorous model selection, assumption testing, and results that hold up under scrutiny.
Statistical Modeling
Forecasting, demand modeling, and quantitative analysis — with a focus on interpretability alongside technical correctness.
Statistical Education
Teaching statistical tools in a way that transfers — not just how to run a command, but why the method works and how to defend it.

How I work
01
I start with the problem, not the method.
Tools are chosen because they fit the question — not because they are impressive or familiar.
02
I document everything I build.
A pipeline or system that only I can maintain is not a deliverable — it is a dependency. Every engagement ends with clear documentation.
03
I explain until it makes sense.
Whether it is a tutoring session or a project handoff, I do not move on until the person in front of me understands what was done and why.
04
Confidentiality is non-negotiable.
Client data, context, and project details stay private — always. The work I share publicly reflects the methods and depth of what I do, never who I do it for.
